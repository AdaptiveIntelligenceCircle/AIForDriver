# Code of Conduct — AIForDriver

We are committed to building a community centered around **ethical AI**, **safe experimentation**, and **introspective innovation**.

---

## Expected Behavior
- Communicate respectfully and professionally.
- Share ideas and findings transparently.
- Document AI decisions and simulations clearly.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing or demonstrating unsafe control code.
- Using AIForDriver for testing in public roads or real vehicles without permission.

---
